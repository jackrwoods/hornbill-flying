Common questions

Can I visit the school before signing up?

Yes. Stop by the office during business hours or call ahead to confirm someone is at the ramp. Visiting is the easiest way to see the aircraft, meet the team, and ask questions before you commit.

Do I need an appointment for a discovery flight?

Yes, discovery flights are scheduled by appointment so we can pair you with an instructor and aircraft. Book online or call the office to reserve a time that fits your schedule.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

Bring a government-issued photo ID and comfortable clothing. Sunglasses and a notebook are helpful. We handle the aircraft, fuel, and preflight briefing.

How do I schedule a rental or lesson?

Members and students schedule through the booking system or by calling the office. For ongoing lessons, you coordinate directly with your instructor once you are paired.

Is parking free at the office?

Yes. Free visitor parking is available near the office at 1008 Gentry Way, right on the RNO field.
