## Problem to Solve
Current and enrolled students need a hub for weather, medical, syllabi, and POH access, and the page also supports SEO by capturing long-tail student queries.

## Solution Approach
Build `/student-resources/` with sections: weather links (Aviation Weather Center, local METAR/TAF via widget), medical certificate guidance, training syllabi, POH/quick-reference downloads (or links), flight-planning tools, and useful FAA resources. Link internally to tools/widgets, blog posts, and relevant program pages. Keep content current and verify external links.
