## Problem to Solve
The site needs baseline legal coverage to satisfy payment processors, search engines, and user trust, and to reduce booking anxiety with a clear cancellation/refund policy.

## Solution Approach
Create three pages: `/privacy/` (privacy policy), `/terms/` (terms of service), and `/cancellation-policy/` (cancellation, refund, and weather policy). Keep copy plain-language where possible, ensure NAP/brand consistency, and link from footer and booking confirmation. No heavy schema required beyond base Organization; mark as noindex only if legally appropriate (usually keep indexed with basic info).
