## Problem to Solve
Broken or mistyped URLs should not dead-end; they should guide users back to high-value pages and preserve trust.

## Solution Approach
Build `/404/` with friendly on-brand copy, a search prompt or sitemap link, and clear CTAs to Home, Discovery Flight, Programs, and Contact. Keep it lightweight, ensure it returns proper 404 status in static export, and avoid setting it as the canonical for any real page.
