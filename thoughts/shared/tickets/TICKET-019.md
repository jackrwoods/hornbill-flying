## Problem to Solve
Prospective students have recurring questions about medical certificates, scheduling, deposits, gear, weather, cancellation, and program fit. A central FAQ reduces friction and improves SEO via FAQPage schema.

## Solution Approach
Build `/faq/` as a curated accordion of common prospective-student questions. Cover medical, scheduling, what to bring, weather/cancellation, age limits, cost/time, Part 61 vs 141, and next steps. Implement FAQPage schema, ensure answers are concise and on-brand, and link internally to Discovery Flight, program pages, and Contact.
